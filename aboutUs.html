Our Story

Resin Services was started in 2017 with the goal to support and grow the surfing community in San Clemente, CA. As time went on we eventually opened a glass shop to better serve those we worked with. We love building boards and helping people get in the water to enjoy them.

We are avid surfers and to us, riding waves and surfing is something that we have been given to enrich our lives. A lifestyle that is meant to make us happy and healthy all while sharing it with our friends and family. Our boards are all made with a singular purpose, to ride waves and ride them often. Ride them all! Paipos, eggs, longboards, shortboards or even a wet set of swim fins. It's all about the ride and our boards can help you get there.
