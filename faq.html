FAQs

Your questions answered. Everything you need to know about Resin Services.

What is Resin Services?
Resin Services is a surf-focused brand born in San Clemente, CA. We support surfers by building boards, offering services, and sharing the stoke of the ride.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we offer international shipping for select products. Please contact our support for detailed options and timelines for your region.
How can I order a custom board?
Just reach out through our contact page or visit our shop. Our team will guide you through shape, size, and material options for your dream board.
Can I visit your workshop?
Absolutely! Our shop is open to visitors. Come meet the crew, see our boards in the making, and talk story with fellow surfers.
Glassing and CNC
Resin Services offers glassing and board building services to the public!
What's the turnaround time for custom boards?
Turnaround time typically ranges from 3–5 weeks depending on demand and customization level. We'll keep you updated every step of the way.
Do you repair surfboards?
Yes, we offer repairs on dings, cracks, and other surfboard damages. Just bring your board in and we'll give you an honest assessment.
Can I get a tour of your glass shop?
Definitely. We love showing people the craft behind our boards. Just call ahead so we can give you the best experience possible.
