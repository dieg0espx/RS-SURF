About Us

Resin Services was founded in 2017 to support and grow the surf community in San Clemente, CA. From building boards to launching our own glass shop, our mission has always been to help people get in the water and enjoy the ride.

"Riding waves and surfing is something that we have been given to enrich our lives."

We're not just board builders — we're surfers. This lifestyle keeps us happy, healthy, and connected with our friends and family. Every board we make has one goal: to help you ride more waves and enjoy every second of it.

Whether it's a Paipo, an egg, a longboard, shortboard, or even just a wet pair of swim fins — it's all about the ride. And we're here to help you get there.
